The Full System

Everything Envision can build and run.

Eight manufacturing capabilities and the services that support them, all shipping under contract today. Hover any tile for the overview.

Capabilities
Capability

Polymer & Film Manufacturing

An end-to-end domestic production process — sample development, production, and pack & ship — converting polyethylene film into finished can liners, medical and specialty bags, and plastic sheeting. HDPE and LLDPE across a wide range of gauges and sizes.

Capability

Textile & Apparel

Heavy sewing operations producing Berry-compliant military gear and ANSI-certified high-visibility safety apparel. Gerber Paragon CNC cutting from partner-supplied digital patterns and markers.

Capability

Reflective Safety Systems

In-house sewing and assembly of high-visibility reflective products. Vinyl and nylon received on rolls, sewn together, fitted with buckles and hardware, and finished in house for high-visibility safety applications.

Capability

Binders & Document Covers

Custom binders, presentation portfolios, and document covers out of the Dallas campus — vinyl and presentation award, retirement, hardcover and custom, certificate and document covers, and Texas State Seal portfolios.

Capability

Writing Instruments

Marker assembly and pen lines — dry erase, highlighters, and permanent markers from the Dallas campus, plus pens assembled at the Wichita campus. AbilityOne, Texas WorkQuest, and Kansas State Use catalog SKUs.

Capability

Fulfillment & Distribution

Federal-grade rigor applied to commercial fulfillment. The qualification work is already done: compliant, audited fulfillment that ships anywhere and integrates with your systems, on a federal-compliant labor model.

Capability

Kitting & Assembly

Value-add kitting and assembly that builds finished, ready-to-issue kits from component parts, including military kit builds, on a federal-compliant labor model.

Capability

Specialty Products

Custom decoration and bespoke production. Logo embroidery and heat-transfer logo application on apparel and finished goods, plus specialty product lines built to customer specification.

Services
Service

Print

Web-to-print storefronts with customer-approved templates, instant soft proofs, and configurable approvals — the front door to federal-grade commercial print out of Wichita. Digital and offset production, bindery, and in-house Grade 2 and Computer Braille embossing.

Service

Envision Contact Center

Bi-locational, multi-channel customer service operations built for federal and enterprise scale. Phone, email, chat, and digital support out of two synchronized sites in Dallas and Wichita, on an AbilityOne-compliant labor model.

Service

Secure Document Services

High-security mailroom operations for federal agencies, including IRS mailroom contracts in Dallas and Farmers Branch. High-speed scan, OCR/ICR digitization, encrypted archive, and certified destruction.

Service

Accessibility Services

ADA Title II consulting, WCAG-aligned audits, training, and user testing — with lived-experience expertise on every engagement.

Service

Federal Procurement Services

A managed pathway into federal and military procurement. We handle the channel infrastructure, compliance credentials, and procurement intelligence so manufacturers can sell into government markets without building the federal machinery themselves.

Service

Quality Assurance & Compliance

ISO 9001:2015 across the Wichita and Dallas campuses. Material and dimensional validation against gauge, strength, and spec in the on-site Quality Labs, with DCMA Alternate Release authorized out of Dallas.

Why It Works

A partner built for the long run.

The value is not a single quote. It is a stable, local source that can invest, scale, and hold quality over years, not quarters.

Reliable by Design

Our workforce is built around consistency and retention above industry average. The repetitive operations that strain a traditional line are the ones we hold steady.

Stable Enough to Invest

Financial stability lets us stand up equipment for a program rather than asking you to. Small presses run $100K to $200K, with Bosch dies. We can carry that investment.

Equipment Pathway

As Bosch decommissions older lines, that tooling can move to our floor instead of to scrap. It is a clean way to seed a program with proven equipment.
