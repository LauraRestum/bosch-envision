The Strategic Fit

Your core competency is a unit out the door.

The work that feeds that unit is ours. Envision is built around the repetitive, high-consistency production that is hardest to staff reliably anywhere else. Two halves of one line, not a side job.

Bosch does best
The finished unit
  • Final assembly and system integration
  • Engineering, brand, and distribution
  • The high-value work that ships out your door
Envision feeds your line
Envision does best
The work upstream
  • Repetitive, high-cycle-count tasks that challenge traditional staffing
  • Sub-assembly and component work that feeds final assembly
  • Consistent output where turnover is the usual enemy
  • Local production, one drive from your dock
Sheet metal is not your core competency. A unit going out the door is. This partnership begins exactly where that line points.

Why It Works

A partner built for the long run.

The value is not a single quote. It is a stable, local source that can invest, scale, and hold quality over years, not quarters.

Reliable by Design

Our workforce is built around consistency and retention above industry average. The repetitive operations that strain a traditional line are the ones we hold steady.

Stable Enough to Invest

Financial stability lets us stand up equipment for a program rather than asking you to. Small presses run $100K to $200K, with Bosch dies. We can carry that investment.

Equipment Pathway

As Bosch decommissions older lines, that tooling can move to our floor instead of to scrap. It is a clean way to seed a program with proven equipment.

One local source. Multiple operations under one roof. Every program funds employment for people who are blind or have low vision, a story your procurement and your brand can both stand behind.
